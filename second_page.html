Cell Structure

A cell contains smaller parts called organelles. These structures work together to keep the cell alive and functioning.

  • Nucleus: stores DNA and controls many cell activities.
  • Cell membrane: protects the cell and regulates movement in and out.
  • Cytoplasm: jelly-like material where many reactions happen.
  • Mitochondria: release energy from food molecules.
  • Ribosomes: build proteins needed for growth and repair.
