    Many concerned parents have come to us raising questions regarding their child's vaccine schedule. They express concern regarding the frequency of their shots and whether their child is at risk of being overwhelmed. We want to address these claims and ease parents' concerns.

  • How Vaccines Work: Vaccines work by exposing children to harmless variants of infectious diseases and allowing their immune system to identify and easily fight these infections in the future.
  • B Cells: Able to modify their antigen receptors to fit virtually every pathogen needed to fight.
  • Modern Vaccines: Most modern vaccines carry few antigens, far fewer than 100.
  • Possible Limit: Infants can withstand up to roughly 10,000 vaccines at once.
